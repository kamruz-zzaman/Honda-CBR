Styling

The Pulsar 150 comes with aggressive laser edged graphics with premium new paint. With carbon fiber accents and instrument cluster graphics, there's nothing in the bike that goes unnoticed. Comfort

The Honda CBR-300R has been designed to ensure maximum comfort for the rider with its upright seating position, smooth suspension, contoured seat made with premium material and 43 cm alloy wheels. Resting on telescopic front forks with anti-friction bushes and a 5-step adjustable Nitrox rear shock absorbers, the Pulsar 150 is a delight to handle and a pleasure when riding in the city or out on the highway.

The Honda CBR-300R comes equipped with a 260 mm ventilated front disc brake that ensures a quicker and safer braking each time. The bike also boasts of a light sensitive digital cockpit and 12V full DC headlamp ensuring a constant light beam at all speeds and great day-night visibility. The beefed up frame with 1320 mm wheelbase and tubeless tyres further add strength and stability. With ABS get ultra-safe braking performance on any surface.
